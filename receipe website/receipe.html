PANCAKES


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon Powder

Procedure

  • Add eggs and cinnamon with flour. Mix until combined.
  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add a spoonful of batter and cook for 3-4 minutes,
    then flip and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes.
  • Serve with maple syrup or honey.



BANANA SMOOTHIE


Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Procedure

  • Cut the bananas
  • Add banana , milk and sugar to the mixer
  • Blend until you get a smooth consistency
  • Garnish with some chocolate powder and banana slices on top.
  • Serve chilled with ice.






BREAD PIZZA


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Butter
  • Orergano
  • Salt

Procedure

  • Apply butter on the bread.
  • Add the veggies on the top of your bread slice.
  • Sprinkle salt and oregano on the top.
  • Cover the whole bread slice with cheese and put in the microwave until the cheese melts.



PASTA SALAD


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Mayonaise
  • Orergano
  • Salt

Procedure

  • Boil the pasta.
  • Mix the pasta along with the vegetables and addmayonaise or your favourite dressing.
  • Add salt,pepper and oregano.
  • Top thesalad with some cheese.



MOJITO


Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Mint leaves
  • Soda/Water
  • Sugar syrup
  • Salt
  • Ice

Procedure

  • Squeeze the lemon in a glass of soda.
  • Add salt and sugar syrup.
  • Add some crushed mint leaves.
  • Mix well and serve with some ice.
